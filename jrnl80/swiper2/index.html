Hofstra University Relay for Life 2018

By Kathryn Burba and Jeremy Skiba

The American Cancer Society is an organization that raises funds and awareness for cancer research through their main event, Relay for Life.

This event hits close to home for the Hofstra Community because approx. 35,000 New Yorkers die from cance every year, with numbers that are continually increasing.
The community honors loved ones lost by lighting candles in their honor to light the relay path.
